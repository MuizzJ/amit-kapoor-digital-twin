Component 01

Data Foundations —
Building the Brain

🏢 Business Value
We take your life's work — books, EPUBs, interviews — and place them in a secure digital vault. The AI can only speak using your actual words, guaranteeing authenticity and preventing fabrication.
⚙️ Technical Stack
Data Ingestion Pipeline
Parses EPUB, PDF, TXT into semantic chunks with overlap and metadata tags.
Vector Database (Pinecone)
Stores embeddings for semantic similarity search — retrieves the most relevant passage for any question.
LLM — Claude Sonnet 4.6
Acts as orchestrator: retrieves context from Pinecone via RAG, synthesizes a grounded, in-character answer.
